Welcome to!

Founded at the dawn of the modern travel era,QAZ store is on a perpetual mission to explore new horizons, propelled by an innovative spirit, boundless creativity, and unparalleled savoir-faire.
Immerse yourself in the fascinating stories our souvenirs tell. Each item is a unique work of art, created by artisans dedicated to preserving our traditions. From embroidered fabrics to ceramic gifts, we have something special for everyone who appreciates beauty and authenticity.

Bring a piece of our culture into your home or give a unique souvenir to your loved ones. You will find here not only beautiful objects, but also a piece of our heart and soul.
Thank you for choosing us. We hope that each souvenir will become for you not only a beautiful object, but also an unforgettable memory of your visit to the world of our traditions and hospitality. Happy shopping!
